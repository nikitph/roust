What is Roust?

A majority of urban India is moving to Apartments. Buildings are getting bigger. Consequently both the physical distance and the mental distance between people is increasing. People don't know who lives on the floor below or even on the same floor. Larger buildings also mean more maintenance and logistics. Roust is an attempt to connect residents and simplify property management using the latest in information technology.
